Final Project Proposal

Website Idea

My website is for baseball fans to have easy access to a variety of stats and data.

Intended Audience

The intended audience is baseball fans.

Styles & Design

For this website, I want to give it a more modern look. I plan to use a Sans-serif font that is easy to read and a bit more modern. I am also going to keep the colors a bit toned down so the website is more sleek and not to flashy.

Color Palette

Inspiration

  • Baseball Reference
  • Fangraphs
  • Baseball Savant

Scope

I plan to have multiple pages for the website. The website will have a home page and a dropdown menu with each team in Major League Baseball with each team having its own page containing its players stats. There will also be an articles page that has baseball articles.

Data

Stats of individual plaeyrs will be stored in the database, such as number of hits, homeruns, or rbis.

Baseball reference and fangraphs will be the source of my data.
