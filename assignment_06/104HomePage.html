Best Teams

The best teams currently in the MLB are the Los Angeles Dodgers, Atlanta Braves, and Baltimore Orioles. These teams have a strong combination of being able to get on base and hit for power, as well as having strong starting pitching and reliable bullpen arms.

While the Dodgers and Braves have a mix of new, young talent and old savvy veterans, the Orioles have graduated their loaded farm talent which has led to their recent succes. While the teams may be different, they do one thing the same: win.

Best Players

The best players currently in baseball are Mookie Betts, Aaron Judge, Ronald Acuna Jr, Mike Trout, and Freddie Freeman. These players are both elite on both offense and defense. Mookie and Freeman are both gold glove winners and Trout, Judge and Acuna have all graded as top tier defenders. Still, there is one player above the rest.

This player is Shohei Ohtani. Ohtani is doing something that hasn't been done since Babe Ruth, which is hitting and pitching at both an elite level. This year, Ohtani leads the league in both homeruns and on-base plus slugging. Last season, he was 3rd in Cy-Young voting. Ohtani is easily the best player in baseball and most likely the most talented player of all-time.

World Series Prediction

As of now, it seems that the World Series will be coming down to the three teams mentioned above, which are the Dodgers, Braves, and Orioles. The Dodgers and Braves are both in the National League, so it is possible that one will have to eliminate the other in order to reach the World Series. The American League is weaker this year which makes the Orioles path to the World Series easier, though the Astros, Mariners, and Rangers are all strong teams.

Even with all this talent, I believe that the Braves will win the World Series. They are a team without a weakness. They have the best offense in baseball and one of if not the best starting rotation as well. Their bullpen isn't elite, but it is solid enough for them to be my World Series favorites.
